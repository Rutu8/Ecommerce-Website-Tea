Inception and Overview

Gadre Tea Company, established in the year 2000, has emerged as a prominent player in the tea industry. Specializing in Selection, Purchase, and Blending of best quality teas. With our retail presence in various parts of Maharashtra, our operational hub resides in the vibrant city of Kolhapur.

Commitment to Excellence

At Gadre Tea Company, our mission is to craft the perfect blend of tea that delights the senses and captivates the palate. Our dedication to excellence extends throughout our entire process, ensuring that each cup of tea reflects our unwavering commitment to quality and taste.

Crafting the Perfect Blend

Our journey to creating the perfect blend begins with a meticulous tea tasting session conducted by our expert team. Sampling teas from renowned gardens, we meticulously select only the finest quality leaves to incorporate into our blends. This ensures that every cup of tea we offer is a harmonious combination of the best selections from these esteemed gardens.

Exceptional Tea Experience

With our emphasis on quality sourcing, blending, and craftsmanship, Gadre Tea Company promises an exceptional tea experience with every sip. Whether enjoyed alone for a moment of tranquility or shared with loved ones, our teas are crafted to elevate the everyday and awaken the senses.

Experience the unparalleled quality and richness of flavor that defines Gadre Tea Company. Join us on a journey of taste and discovery, where every cup tells a story of dedication, passion, and excellence.
